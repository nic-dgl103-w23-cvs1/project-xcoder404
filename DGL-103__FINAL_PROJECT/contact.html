Locations

COURTENAY

Curry Cottage is a popular Indian restaurant located in Courtenay, British Columbia. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, it offers a wide range of delicious Indian cuisine, including vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Their menu features classic dishes like butter chicken, samosas, and biryani, all made with fresh and flavorful ingredients. Come and enjoy a taste of India at Curry Cottage!

2002 Comox Ave, Comox BC V9M 3M6

BURNABY

Curry Cottage Burnaby is a restaurant that brings a new twist to traditional Indian cuisine by incorporating Southeast Asian flavors. The menu offers a variety of fusion dishes infused with exotic spices and bold flavors inspired by countries such as Thailand and Indonesia. The welcoming and laid-back atmosphere of the restaurant provides the perfect setting for a casual lunch or dinner with friends. With its unique culinary offerings, Curry Cottage Burnaby is a must-visit spot for food lovers.

4820 Kingsway Suite 352, Burnaby BC V5H 4P1

SURREY

For more than a decade, Curry Cottage, a family-run Indian restaurant, has been catering to Surrey residents. Their dedication to using only fresh and authentic spices has garnered them a devoted clientele. Their warm and inviting setting, coupled with attentive service, makes Curry Cottage the ultimate destination for indulging in exquisite cuisine with loved ones.

16030 24 Ave #130, Surrey BC V3Z 0R5

ABBOTSFORD

Curry Cottage Abbotsford is an Indian restaurant that offers a modern take on traditional cuisine. Their menu features classic Indian dishes with a contemporary twist, such as their butter chicken tacos and paneer quesadillas. The sleek and stylish interior design of the restaurant provides a chic and vibrant dining experience. They prioritize using locally sourced ingredients to ensure that each dish is made with the freshest and highest quality produce. With their innovative approach to Indian cuisine and commitment to quality, Curry Cottage Abbotsford is a must-visit restaurant for food lovers.

114-3280 Mt Lehman Rd, Abbotsford BC V4X 2M9

LANGELY

Curry Cottage Langley, Langley's newest addition to the culinary scene, has swiftly gained popularity among residents as a favorite Indian eatery. Their menu offers a variety of dishes that are both traditional and modern, all of which are made with top-quality ingredients and real spices. The restaurant's modern and trendy decor creates a sophisticated eating atmosphere that is ideal for many kinds of occasions.

146-19653 Willowbrook Dr, Langley BC V2Y 1A5
