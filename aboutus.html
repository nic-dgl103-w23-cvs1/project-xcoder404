STORY

At the Curry Cottage - Tap & Grill, you can indulge in a unique blend of traditional and fusion cuisine that seamlessly combines authentic Eastern flavors with West Coast food. With a passion for spices and fusion flavors, the restaurant takes pride in creating dishes that are sure to delight and satisfy your taste buds. Whether you're seeking a classic Indian dish or something with a modern twist, Curry Cottage - Tap & Grill is the place to be.

At Curry Cottage, everything is made fresh in-house. As a locally family-owned establishment, the restaurant draws inspiration from traditional flavors and cooking techniques. All produce and meat are sourced locally, and the dishes are prepared using cherished family recipes. Each dish is carefully styled with fresh herbs and garnishing to create a truly authentic and delightful dining experience. By focusing on fresh, local ingredients and traditional methods, Curry Cottage sets itself apart as a truly original and authentic Indian restaurant.
